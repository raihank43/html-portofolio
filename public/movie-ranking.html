The Best Movies According to Me

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Vinland Saga

Vinland Saga is a historical anime series based on the manga of the same name by Makoto Yukimura. It follows the life of Thorfinn, a young Viking warrior who seeks revenge against the man who killed his father, Askeladd. Along the way, he encounters various historical figures and events, such as King Canute, Leif Erikson, and the discovery of Vinland. The series explores themes of war, violence, morality, and identity in a brutal and realistic depiction of the Viking age

The Wind Rises

The Wind Rises is a fictionalized biographical film by Hayao Miyazaki, inspired by the life of Jiro Horikoshi, the designer of the Mitsubishi A6M Zero fighter plane used by Japan during World War II. The film portrays Jiro’s passion for aviation, his romance with Naoko, a woman suffering from tuberculosis, and his involvement in the development of Japan’s military aircraft industry. The film also features dream sequences where Jiro converses with his idol, the Italian aircraft pioneer Giovanni Battista Caproni. The film is a tribute to the beauty and tragedy of human creativity and innovation

Doctor Strange

Doctor Strange is a superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name. It stars Benedict Cumberbatch as Stephen Strange, a brilliant but arrogant neurosurgeon who loses the use of his hands in a car accident. He travels to Nepal to seek healing from the Ancient One, a powerful sorcerer who teaches him the mystic arts and the existence of other dimensions. Strange becomes involved in a conflict with Kaecilius, a former disciple of the Ancient One who wants to unleash the dark dimension Dormammu upon Earth. Strange must use his newfound abilities and artifacts, such as the Eye of Agamotto and the Cloak of Levitation, to protect the world from this threat
